# Product Brief

## Positioning

Benthium is a unified operations platform for dive centers and marine activity businesses that need one environment for bookings, customer handling, operational coordination, resources, and reporting.

## Commercial Value

The product is built for operators that have outgrown spreadsheets, disconnected tools, or fragmented workflows across reception, operations, fleet, staffing, and inventory.

## Operating Domains

- booking and reservation control
- reception and front-desk workflows
- diver and participant records
- course and certification tracking
- fleet and trip operations
- inventory and equipment handling
- HR, payroll, and branch administration
- reporting and audit-oriented visibility

## Product Approach

Benthium favors structured workflows, modular architecture, and maintainable product boundaries over feature sprawl or surface-level dashboard presentation.
