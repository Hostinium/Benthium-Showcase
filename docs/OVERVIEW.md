# Overview

Benthium is a business operations platform designed for organizations that run coordinated, multi-role workflows rather than isolated single-purpose tasks.

The product is positioned for environments where bookings, operations, staff activity, resources, customer records, and reporting must stay aligned inside one system boundary.

## Product Character

Benthium is not presented as a lightweight demo dashboard.

It is presented as:

- an operational management platform
- a modular business system
- a candidate SaaS product architecture
- a full-stack Laravel and Nuxt implementation case

## High-Level Architecture

- Nuxt frontend for dashboard, workflow, and operations-facing experiences
- Laravel backend for modular business services, validation, policies, and APIs
- tenant-aware access model for multi-organization thinking
- role-based access control for operational separation of duties
- multilingual user experience with RTL support

## Public Evaluation Value

From a public-facing perspective, Benthium demonstrates:

- clear domain decomposition
- broad operational scope
- maintainable product architecture direction
- alignment between business workflows and system structure
