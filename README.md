# Benthium Showcase

Benthium is Hostinium's operations platform for dive centers, marine activity operators, and service-heavy environments that need unified operational control instead of disconnected tools.

This public repository presents the product at a professional summary level without exposing private source code, internal workflows, or delivery-sensitive implementation details.

## Executive Summary

Benthium brings together booking workflows, reception activity, diver records, courses, fleet operations, inventory control, staffing, reporting, and tenant-aware business management in one modular product environment.

The platform is designed for organizations that run real daily operations and need structure, accountability, and cross-team visibility.

## Who It Is For

- dive centers with multi-step operational workflows
- marine activity businesses with bookings, fleet, and staff coordination
- operators managing equipment, inventory, and customer records together
- businesses that need reporting and operational discipline across multiple roles

## Product Scope

Benthium is positioned as an operating system for business execution, not only as an admin dashboard.

Key operating areas include:

- booking and reception workflows
- diver and customer records
- course and certification processes
- fleet, charter, and onboard operations
- inventory, cylinders, compressors, and supplies
- payroll, staff, and branch workflows
- reporting, auditability, and role-based access
- multilingual user experience with RTL support

## Business Outcomes

Benthium is intended to help operators:

- centralize fragmented operational work
- reduce coordination friction between departments
- improve visibility across bookings, resources, and staff activity
- maintain clearer reporting and operational control
- scale internal processes with a structured system foundation

## Architecture At A Glance

- Laravel 11 backend for modular business services and APIs
- Nuxt 3 and TypeScript frontend for operational workflows and dashboards
- Sanctum-based authentication model
- Pinia state management
- Tailwind CSS interface layer
- modular, API-driven, tenant-aware product structure

## Why Benthium Stands Out

- broad operational domain coverage in a single platform
- modular architecture aligned with long-term maintainability
- full-stack Laravel and Nuxt product execution
- tenant-aware SaaS thinking
- product scope shaped by real operational workflows rather than demo-only features
